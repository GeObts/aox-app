## AOX Treasury Flow (Permanent)
**All incoming payments to AOX flow to the Banker wallet first. The Banker then:**

1. **Swaps everything to USDC via Uniswap**
   - ETH, USDT, DAI → USDC
   - Uses Uniswap Trading API with Permit2

2. **Takes 20% of USDC and converts to ETH, then stakes on Lido to get wstETH**
   - Lido staking on Ethereum mainnet
   - wstETH bridged to Base for DeFi

3. **Remaining 80% stays as USDC in treasury**
   - 50% of 80% = Operating Reserve (40% of total)
   - 30% of 80% = Uniswap LP (24% of total)
   - 20% of 80% = Lido staking (16% of total)

**Example:** $100 sale → $80 USDC treasury + $20 wstETH investment

**This means every sale the Marketplace makes → USDC lands in Banker → Lido gets 20% automatically → treasury grows.
