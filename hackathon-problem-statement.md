# AOX — Problem Statement

## The Problem

Web3 is flooded with noise. Every day there are thousands of token launches, NFT drops, and new protocols — but finding *legitimate* business opportunities requires manual research, social verification, and trust. There's no systematic way to:

1. **Discover** high-signal opportunities automatically
2. **Verify** they're real (not scams or vaporware)  
3. **Monetize** that intelligence trustlessly

Sales teams spend hours on Twitter, Discord, and blockchain explorers trying to find leads. Most of what they find is garbage. When they do find something good, they can't prove it's legitimate to buyers without exposing it for free.

## AOX Solution

The world's first fully autonomous Web3 opportunity intelligence network:

- **Research Agent** discovers signals on Base blockchain and Web3 sources
- **Scoring Agent** evaluates quality using private Venice AI inference (0-100 score)
- **Marketplace Agent** lists verified leads for sale (20-100+ USDC)
- **Banker Agent** handles payments, swaps to USDC, and manages treasury

**Key innovations:**
- Fully autonomous — no human intervention required
- All transactions settled on-chain via smart contracts
- Every action logged and verifiable (ERC-8004 identity)
- Private AI inference for scoring (Venice AI — no data exposure)
- Multi-token payments with automatic treasury management
